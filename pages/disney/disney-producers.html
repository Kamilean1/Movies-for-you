Producers of series and movies on Disney+

Frank Darabont

Frank Darabont is the producer of The walking dead and a three-time Oscar nominee.

Kevin Feige

Kevin Feige is an American film producer who has produced every Marvel Cinematic Universe film since Iron Man and is the producer of The Avengers and Loki.

Melissa Hunter

Melissa Hunter is a writer and producer for the forthcoming Marvel series "She-Hulk" for Disney+. Her previous TV writing credits include "Santa Clarita Diet" for Netflix, "Close Enough" for HBOMax, and "Maya & Marty" for NBC.

Chuck Hayward

Chuck Hayward is known for WandaVision (2021), Dear White People (2017) and Fat Camp (2017).

Gary Kurtz

He was a producer and assistant director, known for Star Wars (1977), American Graffiti (1973) and Star Wars - The empire strikes back (1980).

Jon Favreau

Jon Favreau is the producer of The Mandalorian.

Deborah Chow

Deborah Chow is known for The Mandalorian (2019), and she is on of the producers of Obi-Wan Kenobi.

Sam Moor

Sam Moor is known for Bluey (2018), Nixon's the One (2013) and Tinny (2012).

Alex Hirsch

Alex Hirsch is an animator, television writer, and voice actor from Piedmont, California. His primary claim to fame is serving as the show creator for the hit animated series "Gravity Falls" (2012-2016).

Wendy Molyneux & Lizzie Molyneux-Logelin

Wendy Molyneux and Lizzie Molyneux-Logelin, are American screenwriters and television producers. They are known for Bob's Burgers (2011), Deadpool 3 (2024) and The Great North (2021).

John Walker

He is a producer and writer, known for The Incredibles (2004), The Incredibles 2 (2018) and Tomorrowland: A World Beyond (2015).

Bonnie Arnold

Bonnie Arnold is known for Toy Story (1995), How to Train Your Dragon (2010) and How to Train Your Dragon: The Hidden World (2019).

Robert Zemeckis

Robert Zemeckis is on of the producers of Disney+ 2022 Pinocchio.

Peter Cameron

Peter Cameron is on of the producers of Moon Knight.

Galyn Susman

Galyn Susman is a producer, known for Lightyear (2022), Ratatouille (2007) and Finding Nemo (2003).

Taika Waititi

Taika Waititi is one of the producers and writers of What we do in the shadows.
