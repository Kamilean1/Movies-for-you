Netflix's management announced that in November, the platform will offer a new, significantly cheaper subscription plan. Customers who decide to buy it, however, will be doomed to advertising breaks during the screenings. How Much for Netflix with Ads? In the US, the monthly price for the new plan, known as "Basic with Ads," will be $ 6.99. By comparison, the Traditional Basic plan is $ 9.99, the Standard plan is $ 15.49, and the Premium plan is $ 19.99. Netflix announces that it will broadcast from 4 to 5 minutes of advertising per hour. The 15- and 30-second spots are to be shown both before and during the screenings. In addition, viewers who purchase the cheapest plan will not be able to download movies and series to mobile devices. We believe that thanks to this solution we will be able to provide the right subscription and price for every Netflix fan - Greg Peters' COO told journalists - The most important thing for us is to give consumers a choice. We want them to be able to find out what offer is the best for them. This may mean that some of our current users are opting out of the ad-free plans. The management of the website also assured that it would not broadcast political advertisements.
